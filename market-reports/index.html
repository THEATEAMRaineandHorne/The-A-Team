FAQs

Market Report Questions

Are these reports valuations?

No. They are general market commentary only. A property-specific appraisal is still needed before making a pricing decision.

Why use REINZ sales?

REINZ sales exports help show actual recorded sales evidence, which is more useful than relying only on asking prices or online estimates.

How often should these be updated?

For best accuracy, update suburb reports when new 12-month sales exports are available or when market conditions change noticeably.
