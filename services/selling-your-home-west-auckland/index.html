A Selling Strategy Built Around Your Home

Every property is different. Some homes need strong family-buyer marketing. Some need development potential explained clearly. Others need careful positioning because of condition, layout, consent history, location, or changing market conditions.

Appraisal & Market Position

We help you understand where your property sits in the current market and what buyers are likely to compare it with.

Buyer Targeting

We identify who the likely buyers are, what they care about, and how your property should be presented to attract them.

Method of Sale

We discuss whether auction, deadline sale, negotiation or priced marketing is the most suitable approach for your situation.

Before your home goes to market, our goal is to give you a clear plan — not guesswork. That means understanding value, buyer demand, presentation, marketing, timing and negotiation strategy.

Our Selling Process

1. Free Property Appraisal

We start by looking at your home, recent local sales, current competition and buyer demand. This helps you understand your likely value before making important selling decisions.

2. Preparation Advice

We help you look at your home through a buyer’s eyes and identify what may improve presentation, including cleaning, decluttering, small repairs, garden presentation, staging, photography and video.

3. Marketing Campaign

Your campaign may include professional photography, property video, online advertising, social media exposure, buyer database promotion, signboard exposure, open homes and Raine & Horne Amplify marketing where suitable.

4. Open Homes & Buyer Follow-Up

Open homes are not just about opening the door. We speak with buyers, listen to their concerns, follow up after inspections and look for signs of genuine interest.

5. Feedback & Campaign Adjustments

Buyer feedback helps you understand how the market is responding. If enquiry is strong but inspections are low, or buyers are not taking the next step, we can discuss what needs to change.

6. Offers & Negotiation

When an offer comes in, the price is only one part of the decision. Conditions, deposit, settlement date, finance, due diligence and buyer motivation all need to be considered.

Marketing Designed to Reach More of the Right Buyers

A strong campaign should do more than simply place a property on the major websites. It should tell the right story, create interest, and make it easy for serious buyers to take the next step.

Where suitable, we can also use Raine & Horne Amplify marketing to help increase online exposure and put your property in front of more potential buyers across digital channels.

The aim is simple: better presentation, stronger reach, clearer buyer follow-up and a campaign that gives your home the best chance of standing out.
