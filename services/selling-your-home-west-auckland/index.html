How The Process Works

A Clear Selling Process For West Auckland Homeowners

Selling can feel overwhelming when there are too many unknowns. This process is designed to make each step clearer.

1

Appraisal and strategy conversation

We look at your home, recent sales, current competition and buyer demand so you can understand likely value and timing.

2

Preparation and presentation plan

We help you decide what may improve buyer confidence, from cleaning and decluttering to repairs, staging, photos and video.

3

Marketing campaign launch

Your home is promoted with clear copy, strong visuals, online exposure, buyer follow-up and Amplify marketing where suitable.

4

Open homes and buyer feedback

Buyer feedback helps us understand interest levels, objections and whether the campaign is creating the right response.

5

Offer strategy and negotiation

We help you assess offers properly, understand the conditions and negotiate with the aim of achieving the strongest result.

6

From accepted offer to settlement

Once an offer is accepted, we help keep the communication clear through conditions, deadlines and the settlement process.
