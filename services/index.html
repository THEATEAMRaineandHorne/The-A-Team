Why the right selling advice matters

Every property needs the right selling strategy

A useful appraisal should look beyond a basic online estimate. It should consider the property, the suburb, the likely buyer pool, current competition, presentation, marketing and the best way to create buyer confidence.

Suburb-specific buyer demand

Buyers compare each suburb differently. Family convenience, school appeal, transport, privacy, sunlight, access and value all matter in different ways.

Local pricing context

The right strategy depends on recent comparable sales, current competition, property condition and how buyers are likely to judge the home.

Clear next steps

Edita helps homeowners understand what to prepare, what not to waste money on, how to position the property and when to go to market.

West Auckland Real Estate Services FAQs

Which real estate services does Edita Andrijasevic provide?

Edita Andrijasevic helps West Auckland homeowners with free property appraisals, pricing strategy, home presentation advice, buyer-focused marketing, open home feedback and negotiation support.

Can I ask for selling advice before I am ready to list?

Yes. You can ask for advice before selling to understand likely value, preparation, timing, buyer demand and the best selling strategy before going to market.

Is a free property appraisal the same as a registered valuation?

No. A free property appraisal is general real estate advice about likely market value and selling strategy. It is not a registered valuation.
