Information we collect

When you submit an appraisal or contact form, we may collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • your name
  • your phone number
  • your email address
  • your property address
  • your suburb
  • your selling timeframe
  • any message or details you include in the form

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, provide property appraisal guidance, discuss your selling plans, arrange appointments and provide real estate advice you requested.

We may also use enquiry details to understand which suburbs and services people are asking about, so we can improve our website and local real estate information.

We do not sell your personal information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your details are used for responding to your enquiry and providing real estate-related assistance.

Third-party services

This website may use trusted third-party tools such as form handling, review widgets, analytics, maps, video embeds or website hosting services. These services may process limited technical or submitted information necessary for the website to function.

Google Reviews and external links

Our website may link to Google Reviews, Raine & Horne pages, Google Maps, YouTube, Localo or other external websites. These websites have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by us.

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, provide real estate assistance, meet business record requirements, or continue communication where you have asked for help.

Accessing or updating your information

You can contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to remove your details from our records where appropriate.
