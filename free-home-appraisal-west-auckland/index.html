Free West Auckland Home Appraisal FAQs

Is a West Auckland home appraisal really free?

Yes. Edita Andrijasevic offers free West Auckland home appraisal advice for homeowners thinking of selling, planning ahead or wanting to understand their current market position.

What do I get with a free appraisal?

You can receive guidance on likely value, recent comparable sales, buyer demand, presentation, marketing strategy, possible buyer objections and next steps if you decide to sell.

How is this different from an online estimate?

Online estimates can miss condition, presentation, access, layout, privacy, sunlight, buyer emotion, current competition and local market mood. A local appraisal gives more context.

Do you only help in Titirangi, Blockhouse Bay and Lynfield?

Edita regularly helps homeowners across West and Central-West Auckland, including Titirangi, Blockhouse Bay, Lynfield, New Lynn, Kelston, Green Bay, Glen Eden, Mount Roskill, New Windsor, Glendene, Avondale and nearby suburbs.

Can I ask for advice even if I am not ready to sell yet?

Yes. Many homeowners request an appraisal early so they can understand their likely value, what improvements may matter, when to sell and how to prepare properly before going to market.
