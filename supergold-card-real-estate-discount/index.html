SuperGold Offer Terms And Exclusions

  1. The offer is available to current SuperGold Card holders. At least one registered owner of the property must hold and present a valid SuperGold Card before the agency agreement is signed.
  2. The offer applies to new residential property listings with Edita Andrijasevic - Raine & Horne that are signed during the offer period and successfully sold through the agency.
  3. The 10% discount applies only to the agreed agency commission stated in the signed agency agreement.
  4. GST will be calculated on the discounted commission.
  5. The offer does not apply to administration fees, marketing or advertising expenses, photography, staging, auctioneer fees, legal costs or other third-party expenses.
  6. The offer cannot be combined with another commission discount, rebate or promotional offer.
  7. The offer is not transferable and cannot be redeemed or exchanged for cash.
  8. The discount must be recorded in the agency agreement and will be applied to the final commission invoice when the qualifying property is successfully sold and the commission becomes payable.
  9. Normal agency agreement terms, legal obligations and client responsibilities continue to apply.

Offer start date: 17 July 2026. This is an ongoing offer unless it is withdrawn or replaced. Please confirm current availability with Edita before signing an agency agreement.

Questions about the offer

Frequently Asked Questions

These answers explain the main eligibility and redemption requirements.

Who is eligible for the SuperGold commission discount?

At least one registered owner of the property must be a current SuperGold Card holder and present a valid card before the agency agreement is signed.

What does the 10% discount apply to?

It applies only to the agreed agency commission recorded in the signed agency agreement. GST is then calculated on the discounted commission.

When will I receive the discount?

The discount will be recorded in the agency agreement and applied to the final commission invoice when the qualifying property is successfully sold and the commission becomes payable.

Does the discount cover marketing and other selling costs?

No. It does not apply to administration fees, advertising, photography, staging, auctioneer fees, legal expenses or other third-party costs.

Can I combine this with another commission promotion?

No. This offer cannot be combined with another commission discount, rebate or promotional offer.

Do I need to enter my SuperGold Card number online?

No. Please do not submit your card number online. You only need to present a valid card to Edita before the agency agreement is signed.
