Frequently Asked Questions

West Auckland Property Appraisal FAQs

These are common questions homeowners ask before requesting a free property appraisal with Edita Andrijasevic from Raine & Horne.

Is the property appraisal really free?

Yes. Edita Andrijasevic and her team from Raine & Horne offer free property appraisal advice for homeowners thinking of selling, planning ahead or wanting to understand their current market position.

What do I get with a free appraisal?

You can receive guidance on likely value, recent comparable sales, buyer demand, presentation, marketing strategy, possible buyer objections and next steps if you decide to sell.

How is this different from an online estimate?

Online estimates can miss condition, presentation, access, layout, privacy, sunlight, buyer emotion, current competition and local market mood. Edita looks at the whole selling picture.

Do you only work in Titirangi, Blockhouse Bay and Lynfield?

Edita Andrijasevic regularly helps homeowners across West and Central Auckland, including Titirangi, Blockhouse Bay, Lynfield, New Lynn, Green Bay, Glen Eden, Mount Roskill, New Windsor, Glendene, Avondale and nearby suburbs.

Can I ask for advice even if I am not ready to sell yet?

Yes. Many homeowners request an appraisal early so they can understand their likely value, what improvements may matter, when to sell and how to prepare properly before going to market.
