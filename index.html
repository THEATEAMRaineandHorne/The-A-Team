Questions sellers ask

West Auckland Property Appraisal FAQs

Is the property appraisal really free?

Yes. We offer free property appraisal advice for homeowners thinking of selling, planning ahead or wanting to understand their current market position.

What do I get with a free appraisal?

You can receive guidance on likely value, recent comparable sales, buyer demand, presentation, marketing strategy, possible buyer objections and next steps if you decide to sell.

How is this different from an online estimate?

Online estimates can miss condition, presentation, access, layout, privacy, sunlight, buyer emotion, current competition and local market mood. We look at the whole selling picture.

Do you only work in Titirangi, Blockhouse Bay and Lynfield?

We regularly help homeowners across nearby West and Central Auckland suburbs including New Lynn, Green Bay, Glen Eden, Mount Roskill, New Windsor, Glendene and Avondale.
