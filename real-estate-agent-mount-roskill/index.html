Frequently Asked Questions

Mount Roskill Real Estate Service — Common Questions

Practical information about appraisals, campaigns, marketing and working with Edita.

What services does Edita provide to Mount Roskill homeowners?

Edita helps Mount Roskill homeowners with a local property appraisal, sale preparation, campaign planning, professional marketing, open homes, buyer follow-up, vendor reporting, offer management and negotiation.

How does a free Mount Roskill property appraisal work?

Edita visits the property, discusses your goals and reviews relevant settled sales, current competition, property condition, title, land, layout, access, parking and likely buyer appeal. You receive a realistic market range and recommended next steps without an obligation to list.

Which method of sale can be used for a Mount Roskill property?

The suitable method depends on the property, buyer depth, timing and current competition. Options can include price by negotiation, deadline sale, auction or another agreed approach. Edita explains the advantages and trade-offs before you decide.

How will my Mount Roskill home be marketed?

A campaign can include professional photography, property copy, major real estate portals, database contact, social promotion, Raine & Horne Amplify, signboard, open homes and direct buyer follow-up. The final plan is tailored to your property and budget.

Will Edita work with another agency if they have a buyer?

Yes. Where it is permitted under the agency agreement and another licensed agency has a genuine buyer, Edita is open to conjunctional cooperation when it supports the seller’s interests.

Which areas near Mount Roskill does Edita also serve?

Edita also works across Lynfield, New Windsor, Blockhouse Bay, Green Bay, New Lynn, Titirangi, Glen Eden, Kelston, Glendene, Avondale and surrounding West Auckland suburbs.
